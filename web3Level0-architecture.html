Nirmund (Web3Level0) Architecture

No Cryptocurrencies outside Regulatory framework. Focus on using Web3 methods to improve regulatory framework and trust.

A practical approach to achieve decentralization while retaining the benefits of centralization in a democratic way.

Nirmund – Translates to “One without head”

This can be interpreted as a state of being aware of everything and entering the ‘pass on the information & expect local action’ mode.

Decentralization of social
decisions

A system of circles – What happens in a circle stays in the circle, acted upon and shared based on collective decisions. Meanwhile, designers with access to depersonalized
information enable inter-disciplinary exchanges so that every Circle has the access to the finest intelligence required for decision making.

Circa 2021-22, Circles will be defined by geographical delimiters. When the community decides, we will move to defining circles based on other criteria.

L0 (Level0) System will be based on the District-Code for the Indian Sphere. We envision the creation of approximately 720 Circles (Disdecos) in 2022.

Web3Level0 Architecture

Circles are off-chain DAOs with seamless on-chain governance.
Each Circle will have the following software layers:

Web2 Integration Layer

Integration of the Circles ecosystem with Web2 applications (eg: Banks)

Business layer

Orchestration of App layer

App layer

All digital experiences

Core Layer

OLTP system (ensures a Database accessible to entire Circle is updated in shortest time)

Data Layer

Analytics Layer

Analytics Layer
