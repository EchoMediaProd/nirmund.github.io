Pioneering Web3 as a Social Revolution

Our Story

COVID-19 accelerated our need for non-coercive and non-hierarchical social relationships in our collective pursuit of creative fulfilment.

Nirmund Trust was setup to become the organizing entity of a network of DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations), each with their own management styles and sharing a common commitment to researching, exploring and presenting ideas for societal change which leverage and adapt existing knowledge and technologies to develop solutions that benefit the commons.

Step 1: Awakening concern in the Community

Telling people about the losses to their community when they lose their data sovereignty and informing them about Local Digital Platforms that are community owned and governed.

Step 2: Organizing Community Participation

Organizing events bringing together a diverse set of people across the community and instituting structures for local action and investment.

Step 3: Moving toward sustainable Governance

Enabling the creation of Governance frameworks toward the preservation of the community’s assets including data assets.
