About Us

Nirmund Trust is an organizing entity of a network of DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations), each with their own management styles and sharing a common commitment to researching, exploring and presenting ideas for societal change which leverage and adapt existing knowledge and technologies to develop solutions that benefit the commons.
