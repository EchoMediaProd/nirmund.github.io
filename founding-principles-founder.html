Activating Purpose, Awakening Intelligence #Web3Level0

Activating Purpose, Awakening Intelligence (ap.ai) is an investment thesis which envisions a societal transition from Centralized Industrial structures to Decentralized post-industrial structures driven primarily by a demand shift from scarce physical wealth to abundant digital wealth.

The Thesis, The Purpose – Democratize Digital for a more equitable wealth distribution

21st century money increasingly consists of electronic pulses. It is instantly transferred and monitored on a video screen. It is, in fact, virtually a video phenomenon itself. Blinking, flashing, whizzing across the planet, 21st century money is information – the basis of knowledge.

Work in the 21st century has become knowledge dependent and can be crudely described as the assembly, distribution and broadcasting of information. With money and work moving in the same direction, the entire economy has become “super-symbolic” aka Digital.

Democratizing the means of production i.e. Digital tools is both our thesis and our purpose. We believe that Web3 has the ability to accelerate wealth creation without barriers.

Solution

We are re-imagining the digital landscape into infinite, intersecting Circles

Solution – “Their Circles” – Self-reliant digital communities collectively managed by trust driven principles.

The Digital Platform enables:

1.

All participants to identify themselves & develop trust within the circle (Multiple roles per participant)

2.

Ability to create and manage groups based on consensus

3.

Ability to create and share content including self-promotion

4.

Ability to form markets and transact business

5.

Creation of governance rules in an ownerless, collective operated environment

6.

Each Circle is independent, with ability to communicate and collaborate with other circles
